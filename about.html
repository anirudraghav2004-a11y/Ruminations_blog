About

This is a place for writing that doesn't belong anywhere else. Notes toward opinions. Observations that didn't fit into conversations. Thinking done in public, without any particular obligation to arrive somewhere.

My name is Anirud Raghav. I read widely and shallowly, which I have decided to treat as a disposition rather than a failing.

There is no schedule here, no theme, no niche. Only the occasional compulsion to write something down.
